Chanel Luxury Investments
Our Story & Platform

01 — Heritage Our Heritage & Vision

Chanel Luxury Investments represents a paradigm shift in how individuals interact with the world's most prestigious fragrance markets. Founded on the principles of exclusivity, transparency, and sustainable growth, our platform bridges the gap between everyday investors and the elite world of high-end perfume manufacturing. Our vision is to democratize luxury asset ownership, allowing you to co-own exclusive production batches of world-renowned fragrances.

02 — Market The Luxury Market Advantage

Historically, the luxury fragrance market has been a walled garden, reserved for institutional investors and global conglomerates. However, the demand for premium, artisanal, and designer perfumes has seen an unprecedented, resilient upward trajectory. By investing in our curated production batches, you are backing tangible, high-demand assets that consistently outperform traditional financial markets.

03 — Ecosystem How Our Ecosystem Works

Our business model is straightforward yet highly effective. When you fund a perfume collection through our Boutique, your capital directly finances the production of exclusive fragrance batches. As these batches are produced, marketed, and sold globally at premium retail prices, the generated wholesale profits are distributed back to you daily. This ensures a consistent, passive income stream.

04 — Security Security & Trust

Your financial security is our paramount concern. We employ state-of-the-art encryption and a rigorously audited financial structure to ensure that your principal investment is 100% safe. Every transaction, daily profit distribution, and withdrawal is processed with absolute transparency, backed by our 24/7 dedicated customer support team.

How to use Chanel

Your complete guide to transactions

How to Deposit

  1. Go to the Home page and click on Deposit.
  2. Select your preferred Payment Method (e.g., CBE, Telebirr).
  3. Copy the displayed Account Number and transfer the exact amount using your bank app or USSD.
  4. Enter the Amount you transferred and the Transaction Reference ID (TID) in the deposit form.
  5. Click Submit Deposit and wait for admin approval (usually within 5-10 minutes).

How to Withdraw

  1. First, link your payout account by going to Profile > Link Bank.
  2. Ensure you have a minimum balance of -- ETB.
  3. Go to the Home page and click on Withdraw.
  4. Enter the Amount you wish to withdraw and your Login Password for security.
  5. Click Request Payout. Funds will be sent to your linked bank account upon review.
